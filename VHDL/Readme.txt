1. alu.vhd is the main design (8-bit) and alu_tb.vhd is its testbench.
2. alu_16bit.vhd is the main design expanded to 16 bits and alu_16bit_tb.vhd is its testbench.
3. alu_pipeline_16bit.vhd is the 16-bit design with pipelining and alu_pipeline_tb.vhd is its testbench.
4. alu_accumulate.vhd is the 16-bit design with pipelining and multiply-accumulate and alu_accumulate_tb.vhd is its testbench.
